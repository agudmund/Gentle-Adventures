# Code of Conduct

## The Pledge

Intricate is built For Enjoying. That clause sits in the header of every source file in this repository, and it applies just as firmly to the people around the project as it does to the software itself.

We as contributors, maintainers, and guests pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation. A gallery welcomes every visitor; nobody should need armour to walk through it.

## The Standards

Behavior that keeps this a place worth visiting:

* Welcoming and inclusive language, offered as an invitation rather than a demand.
* Respect for differing viewpoints and experiences. Different minds are different floors of the building, each with a fresh view.
* Accepting constructive feedback gracefully, and giving it the same way. Critique the work, nurture the person.
* Focusing on what is best for the community and the craft.
* Naming genuine inspirations and crediting others' work cleanly.
* Empathy toward everyone the project touches.

Behavior that has no place here:
* Sexualized language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances of any kind.
* Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
* Public or private harassment.
* Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission.
* Any other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting, or that turns the gallery into a trench.

## The Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying these standards and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instance of unacceptable behavior.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and to temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for behaviors they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful. Corrective action here follows the same principle as everything else in this codebase: it exists to preserve something fragile and worth preserving, not to punish.

## The Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, and it also applies when an individual is representing the project or its community in public spaces. Representation includes using an official project channel, posting via an official account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue in this repository or by contacting the maintainer directly ([@agudmund](https://github.com/agudmund)). All reports will be reviewed and investigated, and will result in a response deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. Confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident will be maintained.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by the project's leadership.
